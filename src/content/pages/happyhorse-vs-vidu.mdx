## 30-second verdict

HappyHorse and Vidu represent two paths in the Chinese AI video model race. Vidu, backed by Shengshu Technology and Tsinghua University research, took the methodical route — publishing papers, iterating publicly, and building a user base over time. HappyHorse went viral overnight with an anonymous launch and immediately topped the leaderboard. If you want the model with the current quality edge and the buzz, that is HappyHorse. If you want the model with a transparent team, research pedigree, and more established access, that is Vidu.

## Key comparison dimensions

### Quality

HappyHorse's 15B-parameter transformer with 8-step denoising currently ranks above Vidu on the Artificial Analysis leaderboard. Vidu has been competitive in this space since 2024, with its U-ViT architecture producing strong results, but current signals put HappyHorse ahead on raw output quality.

### Research transparency

Vidu's team has published technical details about their architecture and approach, giving the research community visibility into how the model works. HappyHorse launched with no paper, no named team, and no detailed technical disclosure beyond leaks about its 15B-parameter architecture. For users or organizations that care about understanding what they are using, Vidu is more transparent.

### Access

Vidu has an established platform with web-based access and has been iterating on user experience. HappyHorse's access model is still forming. Vidu is the more straightforward option if you need to generate video today.

### Ecosystem position

Both models exist in the competitive Chinese AI video ecosystem alongside Kling, Seedance, Hailuo, and Jimeng. Vidu has carved a niche through its academic pedigree. HappyHorse has carved one through sheer performance and mystery. They compete for overlapping users but approach credibility from different angles.

## What can be compared with confidence

- Both are Chinese-developed AI video generation models with strong capabilities.
- HappyHorse leads on the Artificial Analysis leaderboard as of April 2026.
- Vidu has more organizational transparency and a longer public track record.
- The Chinese AI video model space is highly competitive with rapid iteration cycles.

## What should stay cautious

- Whether HappyHorse's quality lead is durable as Vidu continues to update
- How much Vidu's academic backing translates to real-world output advantages
- Any claim about either model's training data or specific capability coverage
- Long-term availability of either model outside China
