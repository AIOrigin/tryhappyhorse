## 30-second verdict

HappyHorse and Hailuo AI represent different strategies for winning in AI video generation. Hailuo, built by the well-funded MiniMax team, has invested in global accessibility, cinematic features like Director Mode, and a polished consumer interface. HappyHorse dropped in anonymously and won on raw benchmark performance. If you want to generate high-quality video right now with a working product, Hailuo is globally available and actively maintained. If you care about leaderboard-topping quality and are willing to navigate uncertainty, HappyHorse has the current performance edge.

## Key comparison dimensions

### Quality

HappyHorse ranks above Hailuo on the Artificial Analysis leaderboard. Its 15B-parameter model with 8-step denoising appears to produce stronger results on the metrics that tracker evaluates. Hailuo, however, has earned praise for cinematic quality and motion naturalism in its own right, particularly with controlled camera movements.

### Creative control

Hailuo's Director Mode gives users explicit control over camera angles, movements, and framing — a feature that appeals to creators who want cinematic results without manual editing. HappyHorse's creative control features are less publicly documented. For users who need to direct the "camera" in their AI-generated videos, Hailuo has a clear feature advantage.

### Global access

Hailuo AI is available worldwide through its English-language web interface, with active efforts to build an international user base. HappyHorse's access path is unclear, especially for users outside China. This is not a minor difference — it determines whether you can actually use the model.

### Backing and longevity

MiniMax, Hailuo's parent company, has raised substantial venture funding and has multiple AI products across text, image, and video. This suggests staying power. HappyHorse's anonymous launch and suspected Alibaba Taotian Group connection suggests resources exist, but the organizational structure is opaque, making long-term support harder to predict.

## What can be compared with confidence

- Both are Chinese AI video generation models with strong capabilities.
- Hailuo is globally accessible with a polished interface and known features.
- HappyHorse leads on the Artificial Analysis leaderboard.
- Hailuo's Director Mode is a differentiated creative control feature.

## What should stay cautious

- Whether HappyHorse will develop creative control tools comparable to Director Mode
- The depth of any Alibaba connection between the two models
- Direct quality comparisons on identical prompts
- Long-term support predictions for HappyHorse given its anonymous launch
