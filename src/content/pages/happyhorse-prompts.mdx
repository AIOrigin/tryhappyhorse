## A simple prompt formula

If you want a reusable structure, keep it simple:

1. Subject
2. Setting
3. Motion
4. Camera move
5. Lighting and mood
6. Duration or format hint

That formula helps you write tighter prompts without pretending there is one official HappyHorse command format everyone should copy.

## What makes a prompt feel more HappyHorse-style

The common thread is usually not a secret syntax. It is a mix of vivid subject detail, clear motion, and cinematic direction. Strong prompts usually name the scene, the shot movement, the look, and the pacing in plain language. That is why this page stays transferable instead of claiming there is a verified official HappyHorse prompt interface.
