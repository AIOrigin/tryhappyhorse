# Day 3 / 7 / 14 / 30 Review Checklist

Use this checklist for every published trend page set.

## Required review checks

Every review pass must check:

1. index status
2. sitemap inclusion
3. canonical path accuracy
4. internal links and related-page coverage
5. title and description performance
6. first-paragraph answer quality
7. CTA visibility and next-step clarity
8. conversion or generation-flow health
9. fact-status accuracy and disclaimer visibility
10. uniqueness vs nearby pages

## Day 3

Focus on publish hygiene and crawl readiness.

1. confirm the page is in the sitemap
2. confirm internal links point to it from the homepage or relevant hub and at least 2 related pages
3. confirm canonical path, route, and page title still match the intended query
4. confirm the intro answers the query immediately
5. confirm the disclaimer is visible and non-official positioning is still clear

## Day 7

Focus on indexation and first search response.

1. check whether the page is indexed
2. check early impressions and click signals
3. review title and description quality if impressions exist but clicks are weak
4. verify the CTA is visible above the fold and still points to the right next step
5. verify the page still feels meaningfully different from related pages

## Day 14

Focus on keep, revise, or stop decisions.

1. if there is still no index, fix title, links, sitemap coverage, and uniqueness
2. if indexed but there are no clicks, revise title and description
3. if there are clicks but weak conversion, revise CTA and landing flow
4. check whether fact uncertainty, brand risk, or overlap has increased enough to stop expansion

## Day 30

Make the long-tail decision.

1. keep and expand pages that show impressions, clicks, or conversion traction
2. add variants or comparison follow-ons only when they have distinct intent and value
3. reduce effort or archive pages that still have no index, no meaningful impressions, or no usable information gain
4. record the winning title pattern, CTA pattern, and route relationships for the next trend

## Output to record after each review

Write down:

1. page reviewed
2. day marker, 3, 7, 14, or 30
3. traffic and index notes
4. CTA and conversion notes
5. decision, keep, revise, expand, or archive
